ATLAS-Style Argumentative Writing Rubric (Simple Version)
Total: 10 points (4 + 4 + 2)

Domain 1 — Purpose, Focus, and Organization (0–4)
Does the essay take a clear position, stay on topic from start to finish, and organize its ideas with a logical structure (introduction, body paragraphs, conclusion, transitions)?

Domain 2 — Evidence and Elaboration (0–4)
Does the essay support its claim with relevant reasons, specific evidence (facts, examples, sources, expert opinion), and clear elaboration that explains why the evidence matters?

Domain 3 — Conventions of Standard English (0–2)
Does the essay use correct grammar, spelling, punctuation, capitalization, and sentence structure with few errors that do not interfere with meaning?
