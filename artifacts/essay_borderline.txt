School Should Start Later

A lot of middle school kids are tired all the time. They have to wake up super early to get to school, and by the time they sit down in first period they are basically zombies. I think middle schools should start later because students will be more awake and do better, even though some people will say it is hard to change the schedule.

The biggest reason is that teenagers need more sleep than they get. Doctors have said that kids our age need around 9 hours but most kids don't get that much. When you don't sleep enough you can't pay attention, you forget things, and you might even fall asleep in class. I have seen kids put their head down during a math lesson and the teacher gets mad but its really not their fault. If school started later they could actually sleep until their body wanted to wake up.

Another reason is grades. There was a study somewhere that showed when schools start later students do better on tests. It makes sense, because if you are awake you can think better. Also kids are less grumpy when they have slept enough and that makes the classroom a better place. Teachers would probably like it too because they wouldn't have to keep telling kids to wake up.

Some people say it is too hard to change the schedule. They worry about busses and after school sports and parents getting to work. These are real problems but other places have figured it out so we can too. We just have to plan it.

In conclusion middle schools should start later. Kids will be more awake, get better grades, and feel better. The schedule problems can be solved if people work together. Sleep is important and school should fit the kids, not the other way around.
