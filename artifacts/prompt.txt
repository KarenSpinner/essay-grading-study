Writing Prompt (Grade 8, Argumentative)

Many middle schools in the United States start before 8:00 a.m. Sleep researchers and pediatric organizations have argued that this start time is too early for adolescents and that schools should begin later in the morning. Others argue that later start times create scheduling problems for families, transportation, and after-school activities.

Write an argumentative essay in which you take a position on whether middle schools should start the school day later in the morning. Support your claim with reasons and evidence. Address at least one counterargument. Use a formal tone, organize your ideas clearly, and follow the conventions of standard written English.
